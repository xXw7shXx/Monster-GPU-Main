Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2, 2026

Gamer Alert ("we," "our," or "the Bot") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect and manage data when you interact with our bot on social media platforms and messaging apps.

1. Data Collection Methods

To provide gaming alerts, Gamer Alert collects the following information provided by your host platform (Telegram, TikTok, etc.):

  • Unique Identifiers: We store your Platform User ID and Server/Group IDs to ensure alerts reach the correct destination.
  • Message Content: We read incoming messages specifically to identify "trigger commands" (e.g., /search or /free). We do not store the content of private conversations beyond the immediate command processing.
  • Metadata: This includes your preferred language and notification settings.

2. Data Retention and Deletion

Strict Retention Policy: We only retain personal data (Platform IDs and Preferences) for as long as you are a registered user of the bot. If the bot is removed from a server or a user blocks the bot, associated configuration data is marked for deletion.

Your Right to Deletion: You have the absolute right to request the complete removal of your data from our systems. To request deletion, please contact us at support@w7sh.us. Upon receiving your request, we will purge your user record from our database within 30 days.

3. No Selling of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your data is used exclusively to facilitate the gaming alert services you have requested.
